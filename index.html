Our Story

Every flavor whispers royal heritage.

Established in 1976, Persian Darbar is a celebration of rich culinary heritage, where time-honored recipes meet royal flavors. Inspired by the grandeur of Mughlai kitchens, each dish is crafted with handpicked spices, slow-cooked techniques, and authentic aromas. From legendary biryanis to indulgent curries, every preparation reflects passion, tradition, and excellence. At Persian Darbar, every flavor truly tells a story of taste, legacy, and hospitality.
